Mangos in Daily Life

Uses and a Simple Recipe

Mangos are extremely versatile in the kitchen. They can be eaten fresh, blended into smoothies, used in salsas, or even turned into pickles and chutneys.

Mango Smoothie Recipe

This refreshing mango smoothie is quick to make and perfect for a hot day.

Ingredients:

  • 1 ripe mango (peeled and chopped)
  • 1 cup milk (or almond milk for dairy-free)
  • 1/2 cup plain yogurt
  • 1 tablespoon honey (optional)
  • A few ice cubes

Steps:

  1. Place the chopped mango, milk, yogurt, and honey into a blender.
  2. Add the ice cubes for a chilled texture.
  3. Blend until smooth and creamy.
  4. Pour into a glass and enjoy immediately!
